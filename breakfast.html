What's for Breakfast

The BCBP Breakfast Fellowship is one of the core activities of the Brotherhood of Christian Businessmen and Professionals (BCBP).
It's a monthly evangelization and fellowship event designed to reach out to friends, colleagues, and guests by sharing the Christian experience in the world of business and the professions.
